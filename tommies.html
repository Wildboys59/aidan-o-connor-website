St Thomas Tommies Hockey

The St Thomas Tommies Hockey Program was founded in 1920.

St. Thomas men's hockey has a rich history, particularly at the collegiate level. The program is part of St. Thomas University in Minnesota, which has a strong tradition in various sports. The men's hockey team has competed at the NCAA Division III level and has been known for its competitive spirit and strong community support. The team has had periods of success, including conference championships and NCAA tournament appearances. Over the years, it has produced numerous talented players who have gone on to compete at higher levels, including professional hockey. In 2021, St. Thomas transitioned to NCAA Division I, marking a significant milestone in its athletic history. This shift has brought new challenges and opportunities, enhancing the visibility and competitiveness of the men's hockey program. Overall, St. Thomas men's hockey continues to build on its legacy, focusing on developing players both on and off the ice.
